FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide a free site visit?

Yes, absolutely! We offer free on-site visits anywhere in Punjab. Our expert will assess your space and provide a detailed quote at no charge.

How long does a ceiling installation take?

It depends on the area and ceiling type. A single room gypsum ceiling typically takes 1–3 days. Larger projects are completed in phases to minimise disruption.

Which areas do you cover?

We serve Patiala, Chandigarh, Mohali, Ludhiana, Bathinda, Ambala, Ropar, Fatehgarh Sahib and all nearby areas across Punjab.

What materials do you use?

We use ISI-certified gypsum boards, premium PVC panels, and branded grid systems. Material quality is never compromised regardless of budget.

Do you provide warranty on your work?

Yes, we provide workmanship warranty on all installations. If any issue arises after project completion, simply call us and we'll address it promptly.
